## ‚ú® Key Features Demonstrated

### üé® Voice Design
Create completely new voices from text descriptions alone. Describe:
- **Tone:** Serious, whimsical, cynical, dramatic
- **Characteristics:** Deep, raspy, smooth, gritty
- **Style:** British accent, film noir, cinematic
- **Pacing:** Slow, expressive, calm

No audio samples needed - just describe the voice you want!

### üé≠ Voice Cloning
While these samples use Voice Design, VOX-1 also supports:
- **Voice Cloning** - Upload 10-30 seconds of any voice
- Clone your own voice or narrators you have permission to use
- Perfect for consistent audiobook narration
- Maintains voice quality across hours of generation

### üöÄ Powered by Qwen3-TTS
- State-of-the-art text-to-speech AI model
- Natural prosody and intonation
- Emotional expression and pacing control
- Multiple model sizes (0.6B, 1.7B)
