==========================================
   VOX-1 AUDIOBOOK MAKER - START HERE
==========================================

CONGRATULATIONS! VOX-1 is installed and ready to use.

==========================================
QUICK START:
==========================================

1. Double-click: RUN-VOX-1.bat
   (The app will open in your browser)

2. Read the User Guide: USER_GUIDE.txt
   (Complete instructions for beginners)

3. Keep the black console window open
   (Closing it will stop VOX-1)

==========================================
WHAT TO DO FIRST:
==========================================

In the VOX-1 app (browser):

1. Go to "The Lab" tab
   - Create a voice or clone one from audio
   - Click "Save as Master Voice"

2. Go to "Studio" tab
   - Load your saved voice
   - Load a text file (TXT or JSON)
   - Click "Render Audiobook"

3. Find your audiobook in: VOX-Output\ folder

==========================================
FILES IN THIS FOLDER:
==========================================

RUN-VOX-1.bat       - Start the app (USE THIS)
USER_GUIDE.txt      - Full instructions
Launch-Debug.bat    - For troubleshooting
app\                - Application files
VOX-Output\         - Your finished audiobooks
